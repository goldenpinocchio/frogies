## Variant: Kawaii Night

### Design stance
A darker premium presentation that keeps the frogs cute but gives the rare traits more drama and contrast.

### Key choices
- Layout: split hero plus collector grid, with stronger rarity framing
- Typography: large display heading with a more premium, night-market feel
- Color: deep indigo background with neon pastels, gold, and diamond highlights
- Interaction: rarity filters plus click-to-feature behavior

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: making rare traits feel special and collectible
- Weak at: less airy and less soft than the pastel variant

### Best for
- Rarity preview pages
- Hype teasers
- Community moments where you want the rare traits to pop
